Portfolio 101

What to Expect

The fastest and most efficient way to interpret an artist and their ‘why’ (or reasoning) for making art, is through their portfolios. A portfolio is more than just an accumulation of your best art. It is the continuous story of you, the artist. What is your identity? How does your art showcase that? All of these are important considerations when starting a portfolio. The more in depth you allow yourself to explore, the higher your score will get.

How to Proceed

  1. Pick between a 2D portfolio or a 3D one.
  2. What is your guiding inquiry?
  3. Map out possible pieces for future projects to ensure fluidity between the artworks by connecting the theme of your artworks soundly.
  4. Explore and mess up!
